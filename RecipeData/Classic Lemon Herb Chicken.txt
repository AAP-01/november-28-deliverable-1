- 2 UNIT of chicken breasts

- 3 TABLESPOON of olive oil

- 1 TABLESPOON of lemon juice

- 1 TEASPOON of garlic powder

- 1 TEASPOON of dried thyme

- 1 PINCH of black pepper

- 1 PINCH of salt

- - 1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat and add the olive oil.

- - 2. Season the chicken breasts with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and dried thyme.

- - 3. Place the chicken in the skillet and cook for 5-6 minutes on each side until golden.

- - 4. Add the lemon juice to the pan and let it simmer for 1-2 minutes to glaze the chicken.

- - 5. Remove from heat and allow the chicken to rest for 3 minutes before serving.
